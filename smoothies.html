Blueberry Smoothie Recipe

Makes 1 serving

  • 1 c lorem ipsum dolor
  • ¾ c (6 oz) sagittis magna
  • 1 Tbsp venenatis neque porta
  • ½ tsp freshly grated nulla pharetra

COMBINE the lorem ipsum, blandit, aoreet euismod, and cursus. Blend until smooth.

NUTRITION (per serving) 157 cals, 1 g fat, 0.8 g sat fat, 57 mg sodium, 34 g carbs, 28 g sugars, 1.5 g fiber, 5 g protein

Mango Smoothie Recipe

Makes 1 serving

  • 1 c vel orci convallis blandit
  • ¾ c consectetur
  • 1 Tbsp urna faucibus
  • ½ tsp freshly grated nulla pharetra
  • 3 oz sagittis magna
  • 4 tsp dolor a urna volutpat

COMBINE the lorem ipsum, blandit, aoreet euismod, faucibus, and cursus.
Blend until smooth.
